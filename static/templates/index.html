Overcooked Study

Welcome to Overcooked human - artificial agent cooperation study

Informed Consent form

General Information

The aim of this study is to improve cooperative work between humans and artificial (robots...) partners. We appreciate your interest in participating in this online task. You have been invited to participate as you are between 18 and 35 years old. Please read through this information before agreeing to participate (if you wish to) by ticking the ‘I consent’ box below. You may ask any questions before deciding to take part by contacting the researcher (details below). The Principal Researcher is {{user_config['researcher']['name']}} , PhD student attached to the {{user_config['researcher']['institution']}} and the {{user_config['supervisor']['institution']}} of {{user_config["researcher"]["university"]}}. This project is being completed under the supervision of Pr. {{user_config['supervisor']['name']}} ({{user_config['supervisor']['institution']}}) and Dr. Bruno Berberian (ONERA). You will be asked to play a cooperative game inspired from Overcooked . This should take about 60 minutes. No background knowledge is required. The data will improve the scientific knowledge about the content and the format of the interaction between cooperating human and artificial agents. The anonymised data will be used for validating or unvalidating hypotheses.

Do I have to take part?

No. Please note that participation is voluntary. If you do decide to take part, you may withdraw at any point for any reason before submitting your answers by closing the browser. However, we are only able to reimburse participants who complete all study activities.

How will my data be used?

We will not collect any data that could directly identify you. The responses you provide will be stored in a password-protected server of the Laboratoire Parole Langage and may be used in academic publications, conference presentations and reports for external organisations. We would also like your permission to use the data in future studies, and to share the anonymous data with other researchers (e.g. in online databases). The results will be written up for a PhD degree.

Who do I contact if I have a concern or I wish to complain?

If you have a concern about any aspect of this study, please speak to {{user_config['researcher']['name']}} (e-mail) or his supervisor {{user_config['supervisor']['name']}} (e-mail), and we will do our best to answer your query. We will acknowledge your concern within 10 working days and give you an indication of how it will be dealt with. If you remain unhappy or wish to make a formal complaint, please contact the {{user_config['researcher']['university']}} ethical committee who will seek to resolve the matter as soon as possible.

Please note that you may only participate in this study if you are 18 years old or over.
